Cookie Policy

This is the Typemania.net Cookie Policy. It outlines the website's use of cookies: What they are as well as how and why they are used by Typemania. For additional information, feel free to contact me.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files that help websites in communicating with users' web browsers. Websites send these files to users' browsers who store them until they expire or are cleared manually or automatically. Most modern websites use cookies. They can help a website keep track of a user's movement across different pages on the site, help manage logins, store user preferences, shopping carts et cetera.

Cookies on this website

Typemania.net use cookies for two reasons:

1) Essential website functionality
A cookie is used to handle user logins. By helping the website remember the user, the cookie allows users to remain logged in while they visit the website's different pages. This cookie is removed upon logging out or when the browser is closed.

When logging in, users are presented with an optional "Remember me" checkbox. This setting allows users to stay logged in even if they close their browsers and return later. To achieve this functionality, a cookie is used. The cookie expires seven days after creation, which means the website will remember users as logged in for up to a week, or until the user logs out.

Users' cookie consent and usage tracking preferences are stored in cookies. These cookies also expire seven days after being created.

These cookies are essential to website functionality.

2) Collecting usage statistics
In addition to Typemania's own cookies, the website also uses third party cookies from Google Analytics, a widely used web analytics service. The cookies from Google Analytics track usage statistics, such as which country users connect from, time spent on the website, and which pages they visit.

For information on usage statistics collection (i.e. what data I collect and what I do with it), please see the Typemania.net Privacy Policy.

For more information on Google Analytics and their cookies, please see the official Google Analytics page.

These cookies are not essential to website functionality.
