Instructions

Compete against yourself and others to become a faster typist!

Type sentences as fast as you can for 30 seconds. You may disregard capitalization and punctuation (periods and commas). Potential apostrophes (') and hyphens (-) are required.

To track your scores and compete for the prestigious site High Score, register an account and login.

Notes

Sentences are generated randomly and don't necessarily make any sense, semantically.

This game is primarily intended to be played with a real, physical keyboard, but feel free to also give it a go on your mobile devices.
